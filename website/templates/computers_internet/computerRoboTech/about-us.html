Vision & Mission

To provide quality education to trainee, a well equipped and good infrastructure along with well informed, motivated faculty is crucial. Areas like course curriculum, faculty development and educational infrastructure have been identified as key areas of improvement. Our Mission is to provide engineering solutions to other companies in fields of product design, analysis and layout & bridge the gap between Industrial requirement and Academic education. We provide a platform to learners and equip them with technologies to perform and show their creativity in our workshops and Internship Programs. MoveIn RoboTech believes in extensive full depth training to the students, on the practical projects they want to work upon as per their choice.

Objectives

  • To provides cost effective and quality education at par with the best in the facilities.
  • To develop sense of responsibility, discipline, commitment professional ethics and inculcate sprit of Entrepreneurship among students.
  • To provide the best learning in the field of robotics and automation to the student who are studying engineering in various disciplines and to all those who want to make their career in the field of robotics and automation industry.
  • To bridge the gap between the theoretical and practical learning by providing students in depth knowledge of various machines.
  • To work upon the best and latest technologies in the ever fast changing world and also train students on various modules in engineering disciplines.
